## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

**Browser LocalStorage** (Primary Storage)
- Location: Your device only
- Encrypted: By browser (HTTPS)
- Cleared: When you clear browser data
- Access: Only you (via our app)

**Google Drive** (Optional - If You Enable Sync)
- Location: Your personal Google Drive account
- Folder: `/Prompt Tool/prompts/`
- Encrypted: By Google at rest (AES-256)
- Access: Only you and our app (with your permission)
- Files: JSON format (text files)

**Sync Queue Database** (For Offline Mode)
- Location: Your device (SQLite database)
- Purpose: Track pending sync operations
- Cleared: When sync completes or you clear browser data
- Access: Only our app

**OAuth Tokens**
- Location: Browser session state (in-memory only)
- Duration: Until browser/tab closed
- Never: Stored in LocalStorage or on disk

### 3.2 Security Measures

**Data Transmission**
- All API calls over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
- OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for authentication
- State parameter validation (CSRF protection)
- No mixed content (HTTP/HTTPS)

**Access Control**
- Google Drive: `drive.file` scope (minimal permission)
- Can only access files created by our app
- Cannot access your other Drive files
- OAuth tokens expire after 1 hour (refreshed automatically)

**Data Encryption**
- In transit: HTTPS encryption
- At rest (Drive): Google's encryption (AES-256)
- At rest (Browser): Browser's security mechanisms

**No Server Storage**
- We don't store your prompts on our servers
- Reduces attack surface
- You maintain full control

**You Can:**

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

**Browser LocalStorage**
- Retained: Until you clear browser data
- Automatic deletion: Never (unless you clear)
- Your control: Clear anytime via browser settings

**Google Drive**
- Retained: Until you delete files from Drive
- Automatic deletion: Never (unless you delete)
- Your control: Delete folder anytime via Google Drive

**OAuth Tokens**
- Access token: 1 hour (then refreshed)
- Refresh token: 6 months (Google's default)
- Session state: Until browser/tab closed

**Sync Queue**
- Retained: Until operations completed
- Automatic deletion: After successful sync
- Your control: Clear anytime

### 6.2 Account Deletion

**To Completely Delete Your Data:**
1. Sign out of the app (revokes OAuth tokens)
2. Clear browser LocalStorage (Settings → Clear Data in browser)
3. Delete `/Prompt Tool/` folder from Google Drive
4. Revoke app access: Google Account → Security → Third-party apps → Prompt Tool → Remove Access

**Result**: All your data is deleted. We cannot recover it.
